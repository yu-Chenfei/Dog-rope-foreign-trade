History & Developments

Shijiazhuang Zehuai Trading Co.,Ltd was established in 1985 by

Zehuai an avid canine enthusiast.As a lifelong hunter and dog trainer,Zehuai spent years studying the habits of dogs and their handlers in order to engineer and develop products to suit their needs and enhance the hunting and training experience.Zehuai Pet product lines have expanded to include basics and accessories for everyday dog needs, as well as healthy chew treats and natural treats.

Beginning as a three-person garage operation,Zehuai Pet has expanded operation to over 250,00 sq.ft. of manufacturing and distribution space.We now manufacture and distribute over 200 items and are the most diverse manufacturer in the industry.Much of our product line is made in the China.Our nylon and leather products are handcrafted at our facility in Xingtai City.Many of the killed craftsmen who make these products have worked here 20 years or more.

Zehuai Pet has been a pioneer in the pet products industry. From the cuved buckle which we introduced over 20 years ago, to the adjustable collar,to the suitcase style fold-up crate (all now industry standards),we are always looking to improve processes and inspire innovation.
